Love journey

Our Love Story

2012 - 2018

School Days - Close Friends

From middle school to high school, we were close friends in the same group. We studied together, played together, and shared all the joys and sorrows of our school years. Those beautiful memories created a strong friendship, which became the foundation for our love later on.

2021 - 2023

Military Service - My Confidant

In 2021, I left for military service. Those days away from home, away from friends, away from everything familiar. During that difficult time, you were the only one I confided in, the one who always listened and encouraged me whenever I felt tired. The letters, calls, and messages became the greatest motivation helping me get through 2 years of service. It was during that time that I realized you weren't just a friend, but the most important person in my life.

2023

The Confession - After 2 Years of Waiting

After completing military service, I returned with a clear determination. The months apart had given me time to realize my feelings. One beautiful afternoon, I mustered the courage to say everything that had been building up in my heart. "Do you know, during those 2 years, you were the only one I thought about, the only one I wanted to share everything with. I love you, not just as a friend anymore." And you accepted. From then on, we officially began our journey of love.

2023 - 2025

Our Love Journey - With Ups and Downs

From 2023 until now, we have experienced many beautiful moments together. Romantic dates, trips together, times when we faced difficulties together. But life isn't always rosy. We also had our arguments, disagreements, and moments of exhaustion. But each time, we remembered those difficult days when I was in military service, remembered how you were always there. And then we made up, loved each other even more. Those arguments didn't drive us apart, but instead helped us understand each other better and love each other more deeply.

2025

The Proposal - Together Forever

After everything we've been through - from close friendship, to the days apart during military service, to falling in love and even our arguments - we understood that this is the person we want to spend our whole life with. Under a starry sky, at a place with many beautiful memories, I knelt down and proposed. "You've been by my side during the hardest times, you've helped me overcome every challenge. Now I want to invite you to be my life partner, to continue life's journey with me. Will you?" You said "Yes" with tears of happiness. And from that moment, we knew this was the beginning of an eternal love story, built on the solid foundation of friendship, trust, and sincere love.
